Why Choose GTSS?

🔓

Open to All - No Exceptions.

GTSS is a free, open specification that unlocks new possibilities in traffic signal data sharing and innovation.

Configure Once, Deploy Anywhere

Set it up once and use it seamlessly across any GTSS-compatable tool, platform and vendor without duplication or wasted effort.

Build on the Vibe

Break Vendor Lock-In

Free your data, cut configuration costs, and choose solutions that truly serve your community.

🏦

Reduce Costs

Eliminate custom integration or vendor specific configuration.

Future Proof

A shared standard that makes it easy to evolve, extend, and adopt new capabilities over time.
